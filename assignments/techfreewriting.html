Tech-free Writing Sessions & Journal

Tech-free Writing (TFW) sessions are class time dedicated to writing skills, idea development, drafting, and revision without technology. We’ll also write with technology in this course, but these sessions help us slow down and think more deeply, so we’ll reserve time for that in class.

For TFW sessions, you’ll receive a notebook/journal to use. At the end of each TFW session, the journal is turned in and I will hold it from week to week.

See the Calendar for which class meetings include a TFW session.

Grading

You’ll be graded on thoughtful, consistent participation across all TFW days. Final evaluation happens after the last TFW day of the semester (around Week 14).

  • A: Missed no more than one TFW session*. Participated fully; writing shows thoughtful engagement and effort.
  • B: Missed two or more sessions*. Participated fully; writing shows thoughtful engagement and effort.
  • C: Missed two or more sessions*. Mostly participated; writing does not always show thoughtful engagement and effort.
  • D: Frequently absent, left early, or arrived late. Participation and effort were inconsistent.
  • E: Frequently absent, left early, and/or arrived late. Participation was minimal; writing shows little effort or engagement with the session’s focus.

* One TFW session can be missed to account for illness/life/etc. One additional TFW session can be made up in class on the TFW Make-up Day (see the calendar for the date).
