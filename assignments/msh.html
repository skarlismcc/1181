Macomb Scavenger Hunt

Your mission for the Macomb Scavenger Hunt is to find student resources across campus that will help you succeed in this class and as a student at Macomb.

What you’ll need

  • A phone with a camera (or a digital camera)
  • Time to walk around campus — you can work together, but each student must take and submit their own photos

Part I: Photographs

Take a photograph of yourself in front of the door/signage (make sure the room number or signage is visible) for each of the following:

  1. Academic Success Center — Reading & Writing Tutoring
  2. Academic Success Center — Subject Area Tutoring Area
    This ASC Subject Tutoring area might not have a sign; take a photo that clearly shows the area.
  3. Career Services
  4. Student Life and Leadership office
  5. Another student service of your choice (e.g., Veteran Services, Counseling and Advising, etc.)

Note: Exterior signage (e.g., outdoor wayfinding signs) will not count. The goal is to go inside and either talk with staff about what they do or pick up a flyer/info about the service.

Occasionally, buildings may be under construction and spaces move temporarily. If that is the case, go to the temporary location.

Once you have all five photos, insert them into a Word document.

Part II: Explanation of the Resource

Under each photo, write a brief explanation describing the resource and what services they provide.

Submission Checklist

  • All 5 required photos (see list above)
  • A brief explanation for each resource, placed under each photo

Reminder: Each student must turn in their own project. Even if you took group photos together, submit your own copies and write your own explanations.
