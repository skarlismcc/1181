Weekly Writing 8: In-Class Peer Review

Reqirements

Length: At least 200 words
Genre: Reading Response

Read

4.4 In-Class Peer Review

Write

Respond to the prompts below. Be specific and connect your ideas to the reading and your own experiences.

  • What are your experiences with peer review (have you done one before)?
  • What are your concerns about getting peer reviews? What about giving them?
  • Based on the reading, what can you do to give good feedback?

Grading

✓+ (2/2)
All requirements for the assignment are met, including word count minimums, prompts, clearly referencing the reading (when applicable).
✓- (1/2)
One of the above criteria is not met.
NC (0/2)
(no credit)
Two or more of the above criteria are not met.
