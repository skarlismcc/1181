Weekly Writing 9: Multimodal Texts

Reqirements

Length: At least 200 words
Genre: Reading Response

Read (both)

  • 6.1 What are Multimodal Texts?
  • 6.3 Visual Rhetoric and Digital Writing

The first reading summarizes multimodal texts; the second goes into more detail on elements of multimodal writing.

Write

  1. Briefly pick three main concepts from 6.3 Visual Rhetoric and Digital Writing. Where have you seen some of these concepts used?
    Think about advertisements, apps on your phone, fliers, social media posts, etc.
  2. Focus on design and multimodality.
    Do not focus on writing concepts like style/tone; choose examples that involve design and multimodal elements.
