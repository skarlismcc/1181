Weekly Writing 6: Understanding Visual Rhetoric

Reqirements

Length: At least 300 words
Genre: Short essay

Read

Chapter 6.2: Understanding Visual Rhetoric

Write

Part 1 (short paragraph): Based on the reading, how does visual design affect how we read texts and engage with genres? Be specific and support your answer with examples from the reading.

Part 2:

  1. Choose one visual design element discussed in the reading (e.g., line, shape, color, etc.).
  2. Find a specific communication event from your community that uses this visual element. (See the reminder/definition below.)
  3. Explain how this one visual element functions in your example.
    For example: How is this visual element used? How does it impact the post?
    Somewhere in your writing, clearly identify your community. For example: “This meme is from my discourse community, BookTok…”
    If the post is from a public platform, include a link at the end of your essay. If it comes from a private space, attach a screenshot of the full post, making sure to blur any names or personal information.

Note: Instructions organization updated 6/11/25.

Reminder: What counts as a “communication event”?

For our class, a communication event is a piece of writing, image, or video that is posted in your community. It could be any of the following:

  • A written post
  • A written comment or series of comments
  • A video post (include the caption, if any)
  • An image post (include the caption)
  • A series of Discord or chat messages
  • A reply on a forum
  • And so on…

If your community’s communication events are all text-only, you may describe something about the site’s visuals instead (e.g., a Facebook group banner, a subreddit’s color choices, etc.).
