Project 1 — Genre Analysis Essay Rough Draft

The Genre Analysis Essay rough draft will be the draft that you use for Peer Review. The genre analysis draft must cover the topics outlined in the Genre Analysis Essay assignment page and be at least 80% complete to participate in peer review or receive credit for getting feedback from a tutor.

Rough Draft Requirements

  • Completeness: At least 80% complete (minimum 1000 words).
    The eventual requirement for the final paper is 1300 words minimum.
  • Content: Cover the prompts listed on the Genre Analysis Essay assignment page.
  • Recommendation: Attempt to fulfill all requirements listed on the assignment sheet to get the most effective feedback.
